The Wine History Museum of Armenia is located in Sasunik village, the Aragatsotn region of Armenia, next to the “Armenia Wine” factory.
The Wine History Museum of Armenia represents the rich history of Armenian winemaking, giving an opportunity to get acquainted with
the historical and modern processes of winemaking, from ancient times to the present days. The main exhibition hall of the museum is
located on the 8-th meter depth in the basalt rock, where all the chronological stages of wine development of the Armenian Highlands are
presented detailed due to the rich scientific and interactive materials.
